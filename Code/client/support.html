How Your Donation Is Used

All donations are used to purchase laptops for students in our STEM Accelerator Program. Students in our STEM Accerelerator program, 5-month workshop where 10 successful applicants receive a personal laptop, personal STEM mentorship, are taught advanced mathematics, web development, and given college admissions guidance.

After participating in our STEM Accelerator 100% of our students feel more prepared for their futures in STEM.

The Technology Outreach Foundation is a nonprofit with 501(c)(3) status, and all donations are tax-deductible by law (EIN: 99-4688023).

Based in Memphis, Tennessee. Physical programming expanding to Knoxville and other areas soon.
